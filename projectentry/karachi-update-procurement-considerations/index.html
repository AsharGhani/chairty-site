Home Ration Drive Karachi Drive Planning for Procurement, Distribution, Selection
Tuesday, March 24, 2020

We discussed our plan for procurement of ration packs, distribution strategy, selection criteria for families to helped and what to include in the ration pack.

Procurement

We do not want to just collect money from donors and hand the money to NGOs; nor do we want to buy ration packs and give to NGOs for distribution. That is why we are taking a slight bit longer to speak to a few outlets from where we can source the items for packaging ration

  1. We are assessing the right approach to negotiate appropriate quality (we do not want to go for TOP brand, as we need to help as many families as possible with slight bit lower quality that would cost less; we will ensure in sha Allah that lower quality does not risk their health)
  2. We are assessing the right quantity sufficient for an average family of 6-7 without going over so more families can benefit

In Sha Allah this procurement arrangement will be made final by Thursday.

Distribution:

We have also assessed the areas where ration can be distributed. Those areas are in extreme poverty & slums of karachi. Potential Neighborhoods are: Block 1 Gulistan-i-Johar; Baldia & Mawach Goth (subject to how much is raised & how many we can afford to help)

Also about 20 students from TCF that are identified by the principal, will benefit from our distribution in sha Allah.

Our team will personally get involved in distribution and not to outsource it to any NGO

Mis-Use Prevention:

With respect to ensuring that same household does not come twice (mis-use/double-dipping) we will keep track by registering their addresses and National Identity Card numbers.

How many ration packs/families:

Once final rate is settled & ration packs are procured, I will keep us all updated here on how many families will benefit this month In Sha Allah.
